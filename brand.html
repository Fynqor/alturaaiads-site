Usage Guidelines

Do:

  • Use the horizontal lockup for site headers, docs, proposals.
  • Use the monogram for icons, favicons, and small spaces.
  • Maintain padding around the logo (at least the height of the “A”).
  • Use brand colors shown above for consistency.

Don’t:

  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or apply effects to the logo.
  • Change the brand colors to off-brand hues.
  • Place logos on low-contrast backgrounds without separation.
  • Add outlines or shadows that reduce legibility.
